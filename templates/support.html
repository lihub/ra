Account Security Issues

If you suspect unauthorized access to your account or notice suspicious activity:

  • Call immediately: 1-800-QUANTICA
  • Email: security@quantica.com
  • Change your password if possible
  • Review recent account activity

Platform Outages

If you cannot access your account due to technical issues:

  • Check system status at status.quantica.com
  • Try clearing your browser cache and cookies
  • Use our mobile app as an alternative
  • Call support for account information

Market Events

During significant market volatility or events:

  • Check our blog for market commentary
  • Review your risk tolerance settings
  • Avoid making emotional investment decisions
  • Contact advisory team for guidance if needed
