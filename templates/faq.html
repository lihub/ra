What is Quantica and how does it work?

+

Quantica is an intelligent portfolio optimization platform that uses Modern Portfolio Theory and advanced algorithms to create personalized investment portfolios. Here's how it works:

  1. Risk Assessment: You complete a comprehensive questionnaire about your risk tolerance, investment timeline, and goals
  2. Optimization: Our algorithms analyze 25+ ETFs and create your optimal portfolio allocation
  3. Implementation: We automatically invest your funds according to the optimized allocation
  4. Management: We monitor and rebalance your portfolio to maintain optimal allocations

Everything is automated, transparent, and based on Nobel Prize-winning investment theory.

What's the minimum amount needed to start investing?

+

The minimum investment amount is $1,000. This allows for proper diversification across multiple asset classes while keeping costs reasonable.

Why $1,000? With smaller amounts, transaction costs and fractional share limitations make it difficult to achieve optimal diversification. Our minimum ensures you get the full benefit of our optimization algorithms.

How do you determine my optimal portfolio allocation?

+

Our allocation process combines multiple sophisticated techniques:

1. Risk Profiling

Your questionnaire responses determine your risk tolerance and investment constraints.

2. Expected Return Estimation

We calculate expected returns for each asset based on historical data, economic indicators, and risk factors.

3. Covariance Analysis

We analyze how different assets move together to identify diversification opportunities.

4. Mathematical Optimization

Advanced algorithms solve for the allocation that maximizes expected return for your target risk level.

5. Constraint Application

We apply practical constraints like minimum/maximum allocations and tax considerations.

How often do you rebalance portfolios?

+

We use a smart rebalancing approach that balances optimal allocations with cost efficiency:

  • Threshold-Based: Rebalance when any asset drifts more than 5% from target allocation
  • Time-Based Review: Quarterly assessment of all portfolios regardless of drift
  • Tax-Aware: Prioritize tax-loss harvesting opportunities when rebalancing
  • Cost-Conscious: Bundle small adjustments to minimize transaction costs

Most portfolios rebalance 2-4 times per year, though volatile markets may trigger more frequent rebalancing.

Do you offer tax-loss harvesting?

+

Yes, for taxable accounts. Our tax-loss harvesting system:

  • Daily Monitoring: Scans for tax-loss opportunities every trading day
  • Wash Sale Compliance: Automatically avoids wash sale rule violations
  • Similar Asset Replacement: Maintains portfolio allocation while harvesting losses
  • Loss Banking: Captures losses to offset future gains

Tax Alpha: Tax-loss harvesting can add 0.2% - 0.7% to after-tax returns annually, particularly for higher-income investors.

Note: Tax-loss harvesting is not available for tax-advantaged accounts like IRAs.

We prioritize low-cost ETFs from Vanguard, iShares, and SPDR to minimize total costs.
